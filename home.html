Live Leaderboard For Your Tournament

Enhance your golf outing with a web-browser live leaderboard!

The leaderboard live is a web-browser application that allows golf scoring to be updated in real time, no matter where you are on the course, in the clubhouse or at the turnaround: we'll keep you up to date with any tournament.

How it Works?

1. Create your account. The tournament host can then invite you via your registration email!

2. Join the tournament from the hosts invitation.

3. Enter your scores! The application will then project each score into a live leaderboard at the bottom of your page!
